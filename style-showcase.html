User Guide

💡 What "vibe coding" actually is

Vibe coding is not typing magic prompts.

Vibe coding is designing systems using intent instead of syntax.

Traditional coding asks

"How do I write this in JavaScript?"

Vibe coding asks

"What needs to exist, what should happen, and what should the user see?"

Every modern no code or low code tool works the same way under the hood. They just hide the code and expose the building blocks.

Your job is to learn the building blocks.

✏️ How to Prompt AI the Right Way

Do not say

"Build me an app"

Say

"Help me define the data model for a delivery tracking app. I need tables, fields, and relationships."

Then

"Now design the UI views for planners and field users."

Then

"Now define automation rules."

AI is a copilot, not the pilot.

You steer. AI fills in details.

Break your request into layers: data → interface → automation. This gives AI the structure it needs to help you effectively.

📐 Your First Build Checklist
  • One clear goal
  • One primary table
  • One simple workflow
  • One automation
  • One dashboard

If you can do that, you are officially vibe coding.

Design Principles

10 core design principles for building intuitive, high-performance operations systems.

1

Navigation, Layout & IA

Reduce friction and make it easy to move between views, modules, and records.

  • Unified interface with side panel navigation
  • Simplified landing pages with clear entry points
  • Card-based navigation across projects and items
  • Consistent back/exit actions
  • Phase, status, and category-based filtering
2

Card Views & Interaction

Make cards the primary interaction surface for fast access to detail.

  • Projects, work orders, RFIs as clickable cards
  • Card detail views optimized for compact layouts
  • Drag-and-drop support for files and items
  • File uploads and status updates on cards
  • Quick actions and time entry buttons
3

Dashboards & Analytics

Move from raw data to actionable, insight-driven analytics.

  • Chart and graph focused dashboards
  • Number-first, high-contrast layouts
  • Analytics by project, person, crew, and region
  • Interactive charts with click-through data
  • Forecast vs actual analysis
4

Reporting & Data Exploration

Enable deeper analysis without overwhelming the user.

  • Enhanced reports with KPIs and breakdowns
  • Interactive reports, not static summaries
  • Drill-down by project, person, or time
  • Month-to-month navigation
  • Data aligned with source boards
5

Financials & Capacity

Improve visibility and accuracy of labor, cost, and utilization data.

  • Budgeted vs estimated vs actual tracking
  • Editable labor rates reflected everywhere
  • Time entry buttons on items and cards
  • Approval views grouped by person/project
  • Capacity charts and utilization tracking
6

Work Orders & RFIs

Create clean, fast workflows for operational documentation.

  • Project documentation hub for RFIs/submittals
  • RFI lifecycle tracking and status
  • Fully clickable RFI log with detail visibility
  • Work orders accessible from cards
  • Calendar view for live tracking
7

Risk & Action Center

Highlight what needs attention and what to do next.

  • Risk metrics aligned with underlying data
  • Filtering by category, status, and phase
  • Simplified action center by stage
  • Click-through to resolution paths
  • Predictive risk indicators with next steps
8

User-Centric Views

Help users focus on what matters to them.

  • Assigned tasks view by clicking username
  • Team workload showing capacity/utilization
  • Approval views grouped by individual
  • Role-appropriate entry points
  • Shop managers, PMs, executives, marketing
9

UI/UX Modernization

Make the system feel modern, fast, and intuitive.

  • Higher contrast color palettes
  • Simplified shapes and sharper UI elements
  • Clear visual hierarchy
  • Mobile-optimized layouts
  • Smooth transitions and immediate feedback
10

Commission & Sales

Provide transparency and control over sales performance.

  • Commission tracker by sales rep
  • Editable commission breakdown per user
  • Commission payout module
  • Month-to-month payout navigation
  • Commission data in analytics/reporting
